Cause:
Learn about the Internet.
A misunderstanding that people may have about my cause:
People may be confused about the definition of "the Internet" here. Does that mean the application of the Internet? Or the way of working? Or something else?
Argument:
My cause is to learn about the Internet, which does not mean that I need to learn all aspects of the Interent. I think it is necessary and important to know about the Internet, because more and more people are using the Internet nowadays. But what I can learn depends on the content that the course will cover. For example, in CSC 265, we have learned how the Internet works as well as how to design the webpages using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. They are all related to the Interent and help me better understand what I can do with the Internet.
